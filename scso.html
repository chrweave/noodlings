Scouts' Own for Cubs

This is a quick introduction to preparing Scouts' Own services for Cub Scouts. "Scouts' Own" services are typically deliverd as part of a camp out program, but they are appropriate for any scouting function in which cub scouts are expected to spend some time in reverence.

On My Honor, I will do my best to do my duty to God … A Scout is Reverent

The Scout Oath begins with the reverence that each scout promises to show. The Scout Law finishes with the requirement of reverence for all scouts.
